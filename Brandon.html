Services

Across the board my work showcases a sophisticated and striking aesthetic. And as a practicing perfectionist, I edit every photo I take individually (no batch preset edits), ensuring every image and video from every project I shoot looks its absolute best.

Interior/Architectural

Whether it be for an Airbnb listing, an interior design portfolio, a commercial development, or a magazine editorial, my high end Interior photography will consistently give your project the luster and quality it deserves to really shine. I’m a perfectionist after all so for me it’s all in the beautiful details. And my passion for design and architecture ALWAYS comes through in my work.

Real Estate

With the seemingly endless competition out there these days, making your properties really pop has never been more important. My polished and dynamic images will not only set your listing apart from the thousands of others but will also brand YOU a cut above the rest. After all, a striking image is your prospective buyer’s first impression, so shouldn’t you make the best one possible?

Lifestyle

Let’s be honest, powerful imagery is your absolute best tool these days to catch your markets eye. A professional picture not only brands your business presence with distinction but also puts you in front of a larger (and more engaged) audience. With Taylor’s design finesse and my seasoned photographic talent your business will get the attention it deserves.

Landscape

The unsurpassable exquisiteness of Nature has always inspired me. Attempting to capture this beauty in photos therefore brings me endless joy. I love exploring and I LOVE preserving those euphoric moments spent outdoors in the form of a stunning picture.

Aerial

Aerial photography is quickly becoming instrumental for successful marketing campaigns. Nothing stands out more than a captivating shot or video taken from the sky. My top of the line drone combined with my honed skills will manifest extraordinary results for your next big project.
