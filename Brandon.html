About

Hi There! I'm Brandon

Once upon a time I escaped the crowded clutches of Southern California to attend the illustrious University of Colorado at Boulder. I was instantly entranced by the scenery and culture of our statuesque state and never looked back.

After graduating with two degrees and a couple of accolades along the way, my creative passion began to find its true direction. Jump to 12 years later - with endless professional and personal experiences now under my belt - and I've found my niche.

Above all, I am an artist - a creative force of nature. More specifically - a versatile and seasoned professional photographer/videographer with a vibrant personality to boot. I can't wait to hear from you and get started on our next epic project!

Services

Across the board my work showcases a sophisticated and striking aesthetic. As a practicing perfectionist, I edit every photo I take individually (no batch preset edits), ensuring every image and video from every project I shoot looks its absolute best.

Interior/Architectural

Whether it be for an Airbnb listing, an interior design portfolio, a commercial development, or a magazine editorial, my high end Interior Photography will consistently give your project the luster and quality it deserves to really shine. I’m a perfectionist after all so for me it’s all in the beautiful details. And my passion for design and architecture ALWAYS comes through in my work.

Real Estate

With the seemingly endless competition out there these days, making your properties really pop has never been more important. My polished and dynamic images will not only set your listing apart from the thousands of others but will also brand YOU a cut above the rest. After all, a striking image is your prospective buyer’s first impression, so shouldn’t you make the best one possible?

Lifestyle

Let’s be honest, powerful imagery is your absolute best tool these days to catch your market's eye. A professional picture not only brands your business presence with distinction but also puts you in front of a larger (and more engaged) audience.

Landscape

The unsurpassable exquisiteness of nature has always inspired me. Attempting to capture this beauty in photos therefore brings me endless joy. I love exploring and I LOVE preserving those euphoric moments spent outdoors in the form of a stunning picture.
